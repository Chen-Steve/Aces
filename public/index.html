Game Rules

Gameplay

  1. Place Bet: Enter your bet amount and confirm to start the game. Two cards will be dealt to you immediately.
  2. Hit: If you want another card to try and get closer to 21, click 'Hit'. You can hit multiple times as long as your total is below 21.
  3. Stand: Satisfied with your hand? Click 'Stand' and the dealer will play their hand.
  4. Double Down:Only available as your first decision of each round. After receiving your initial two cards, you have the option to double your bet and receive exactly one additional card.
  5. At the end of each round, hit deal to start again

Betting and Payouts

  • Wins pay 1:1; meaning you win the same amount as your bet.
  • Blackjack (an ace and a ten-value card as your first two cards) pays 3:2.
  • If you lose, you forfeit your bet amount.
  • A draw (or "push") returns your initial bet.
